How it works

{{ site_name }} is a free setlist-prediction game. Before a show starts, you guess what the band will play. After the show, your picks are scored against the real setlist and you climb the leaderboard — on your own or in a private league with friends.

Making picks

  • Pick up to 5 songs, and tap one as your encore call.
  • Picks lock at showtime and can't be changed once the show starts.

How scoring works

  • Each song pick that gets played: 2 points.
  • Your encore call lands in the encore: +5.
  • A pick that doesn't get played scores 0; an encore call that plays outside the encore still earns its 2.
